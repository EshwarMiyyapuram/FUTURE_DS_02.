# Customer Retention & Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing customer retention and churn patterns for a subscription-based business. The objective is to identify factors that contribute to customer churn, understand retention trends, and provide actionable recommendations to improve customer loyalty and reduce customer loss.

This project was completed as part of the **Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Internship (Task 2)**.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze customer churn patterns
* Identify key retention drivers
* Evaluate customer lifetime trends
* Segment customers based on behavior
* Generate business insights and recommendations

---

## Dataset

**Dataset Used:** Telco Customer Churn Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographics, subscription details, service usage information, payment methods, tenure, monthly charges, and churn status.

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Customer Churn Distribution

* Calculated overall churn rate.
* Visualized customer retention vs churn.

### 2. Contract Type Analysis

* Compared churn across contract categories.
* Identified high-risk customer groups.

### 3. Tenure Analysis

* Evaluated how customer tenure impacts churn.

### 4. Monthly Charges Analysis

* Compared monthly charges between retained and churned customers.

### 5. Internet Service Analysis

* Examined churn trends across different internet service types.

### 6. Payment Method Analysis

* Identified payment methods associated with higher churn.

---

## Key Insights

* Month-to-month customers showed the highest churn rate.
* Customers with shorter tenure were more likely to leave.
* Long-term contract customers demonstrated stronger retention.
* Certain payment methods were associated with higher churn risk.
* Retained customers generally had longer customer lifetimes.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Encourage customers to switch to long-term contracts.
2. Implement loyalty and retention programs.
3. Improve onboarding experience for new customers.
4. Offer personalized retention campaigns for high-risk customers.
5. Monitor customer engagement and satisfaction regularly.
